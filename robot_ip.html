Robot and Innovation Project

Our robot is named Boxtoben! It has a cubic design while maintaining lots
of functionality and efficiency. Parts of the robot include the Spike Prime hub,
four bar (or parallel linkage), and wheels + motors.

Attachments

Our attachments are simple and efficient. They slide on with ease and are very
efficient; making handling time during a match not too long. We have 3 attachment types.

  • 1. Four bar/parallel linkage
  • 2. Frames/Skins
  • 3. Front Body attachments
